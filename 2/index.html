Patchouli

Scientific name : Pogostemon patchouli
Family: Lamiaceae
Plant Discription and Health Benefits :The plant grows as a bushy perennial herb, with erect stems reaching up to 75 centimetres in height and bearing small, pale, pink-white flowers. Patchouli oil is an essential oil with many uses, from treating dry skin to use as an antifungal agent.
